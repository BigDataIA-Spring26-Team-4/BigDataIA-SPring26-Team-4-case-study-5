<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">As previously disclosed in a Current Report on
Form&#160;8-K dated July&#160;11, 2025, filed with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission (the &#8220;SEC&#8221;) on July&#160;16,
2025, Kelly M. Dilts resigned from her current role as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Dollar General Corporation
(the &#8220;Company&#8221;), effective August&#160;28, 2025. On August&#160;19, 2025, the Company&#8217;s Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;)
appointed Donny H. Lau, age 46, as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company, effective October&#160;20, 2025,
and determined that Todd J. Vasos, age 63, the Company&#8217;s Chief Executive Officer and member of the Board, will assume the duties
of principal financial officer immediately following Ms.&#160;Dilts&#8217; departure and until the assumption of the role by Mr.&#160;Lau.
Mr.&#160;Vasos will not receive additional compensation for such additional duties.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">Mr.&#160;Lau, who has over six years of previous
employment experience with the Company, has served as the Chief Financial Officer of Zaxby&#8217;s Franchising LLC since July&#160;2023.

<P STYLE="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 24pt"><B>Dollar General Corporation Names Donny

<P STYLE="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 24pt"><B></B></FONT><B><FONT STYLE="border: #E5E7EB 1pt solid">&nbsp;</FONT></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">GOODLETTSVILLE, Tenn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Dollar General Corporation (NYSE: DG) announced the appointment of Donny
Lau to serve as executive vice president and chief financial officer effective October 20, 2025, following the departure of current CFO
Kelly Dilts on August 28, 2025. In the interim, Dollar General&rsquo;s chief executive officer Todd Vasos will serve as the principal
financial officer. Lau previously held roles of increasing responsibility at Dollar General from 2017-2023.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&ldquo;I am excited to welcome
Donny back to Dollar General as our next CFO,&rdquo; said Vasos. &ldquo;Donny&rsquo;s deep understanding of our business, culture and
values and his impressive financial leadership and experience uniquely position him to drive excellence and create long-term shareholder
value. I have full confidence in his ability to guide our financial organization forward.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">Lau will return to Dollar
General with more than six years of previous employment experience with the Company. He most recently served as Chief Financial Officer
of Zaxby&rsquo;s Franchising LLC since July 2023. Prior to his departure from Dollar General in July 2023, Lau served in roles of increasing
responsibility within Dollar General&rsquo;s Finance department, including Senior Vice President, Finance and Chief Strategy Officer (April
2023 to July 2023); Senior Vice President, Chief Strategy Officer (September 2022 to April 2023); Vice President, Investor Relations and
Corporate Strategy (October 2019 to September 2022); and Vice President, Strategy and Corporate Development (March 2017 to October 2019).</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Prior to joining Dollar General, Lau served in various
financial planning, investor relations, and corporate strategy roles of increasing responsibility with Yum! Brands, Inc. from 2011-2017.
Prior to Yum!, Lau also served as Vice President, Investment Banking, with Morgan Keegan &amp; Company and as Vice President, Investment
Banking at Morgan Joseph.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
